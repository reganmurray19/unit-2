ENROLLMENT TRENDS IN THE BIG 10

The Big 10 is America's oldest collegiate athletic conference. Currently, over 600,000 students call a Big 10 campus home. This website explores how student enrollment, both on the graduate and undergradute level, has changed over time. Additionally, users may explore how campuses within the Big 10 differ in ethnic diversity. The diversity index numbers range from 0 to 1 and are derived from a comparative analysis between all American public universities.
